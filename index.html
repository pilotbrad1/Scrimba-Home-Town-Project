Welcome to Ballito

The perfect holiday on the east coast of South Africa.

We have many fantastic attractions

For the golfer

The world renowned Zimbali Country Club is one of the best in the country offering a fantastic golfing experience

For the nature lover

The Indian Ocean is teeming with marine mammal life. Relax on the beach and watch the whales and dolphins playing in the sea

For the surfer

The warm waters mean a wet suit is never required for surfing, canoeing or frolicking in the breakers.
